🩸 THE ROLE WAR BEGINS 🩸

"Earn your name. Or lose it."

A new order is rising.
Your titles are no longer safe.
Your roles are now up for the taking.

No one is untouchable.

You have 3 days to prove your worth.
If you don’t defend your title by then...
It becomes someone else’s — permanently.

To kill comfort.
To spark ambition.
To awaken the fire in every one of you.

🕯️ You were chosen once.
Now... you must choose to remain.

Reply with your agreement and thoughts —
Or stay silent and risk being silenced forever.

This is not a warning.
This is a beginning.
